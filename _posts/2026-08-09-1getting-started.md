# KiCad Beginner Tutorial 2026 – From Zero to Your First Complete PCB

If you are just starting with PCB design, **KiCad** is currently the best free and open-source tool available. In this complete beginner-friendly tutorial, you will go from installing KiCad to generating manufacturing files (Gerber) ready for PCB fabrication — all in one article.

No prior experience needed. Everything is explained step by step with screenshots (placeholders) and real examples.

## Why Choose KiCad in 2026?

- Completely free and open source (no licensing fees)
- Professional-grade features used by thousands of engineers and hobbyists
- Excellent 3D viewer
- Active community and frequent updates
